# Momentum Funding — 26-Week Action Checklist (Path to $1M)
### Companion to `Momentum_GTM_Strategy_2026.md` · June 2026

**How to use:** Each week has a theme, a checklist, and a target. Check boxes as you go. The rule throughout: **never hire ahead of lead supply; prove unit economics solo first.** Two engines run in parallel from Day 1 — **MCA origination** (fast cash) and **VCF debt relief** (big tickets, your $1M lever).

> Legend: 🎯 = the week's #1 priority · 💰 = revenue activity · 🏗️ = infrastructure · ⚖️ = compliance

---

## PHASE 1 — PROVE THE ENGINES (Weeks 1–4, solo)

### Week 1 — Stand up + work your own book
- [ ] 🎯💰 **Pull every declined / stacked / struggling merchant from your CRM** → call list for VCF debt relief (zero cost, highest conversion)
- [ ] 💰 Get **VCF's written rate card** (commission %, persistency, clawback) + their compliance/licensing docs
- [ ] 🏗️ Confirm GHL pipelines live (MCA 9-stage + VCF pipeline) and Sequences A (docs), B (no-answer), F (reactivation) active
- [ ] 🏗️ Create Google Business Profiles for your 6 target metros
- [ ] ⚖️ Set up A2P 10DLC registration for SMS; line up DNC + TCPA-litigator scrubbing
- [ ] 🏗️ Set up call recording + a simple cost-per-funded-deal tracker (sheet or `/admin/unit-economics`)
- [ ] **Target: 5+ VCF own-book conversations booked**

### Week 2 — Live-transfer split test + first UCC data
- [ ] 🎯💰 **Split $1,800 across 3 live-transfer vendors** ($600 each): MCA Leadz, MCA Leads Hub, Lead Slaps
- [ ] 💰 Buy first **stacked-UCC list** (~$1,200, Lead Tycoons $0.175/rec or Klover); scrub it
- [ ] ⚖️ Verify each vendor provides call recordings + written TCPA 1:1 consent provenance
- [ ] 💰 Begin dialing the UCC list for VCF (multi-position = distressed)
- [ ] 🏗️ Log every transfer/lead → outcome in the tracker
- [ ] **Target: 2 MCA deals in pipeline + 2 VCF consults**

### Week 3 — Close + measure
- [ ] 🎯 **Drill the funnel leak: <60-second pickup on transfers + same-day doc collection** (this is what turns 3% → 8%)
- [ ] 💰 Push live-transfer deals through Stage 5 (docs) → 6 (submit to funders)
- [ ] 💰 Submit first VCF files to Value Capital Funding
- [ ] 📊 Review cost-per-funded-deal by vendor — identify the early winner/loser
- [ ] **Target: First MCA funded + first VCF file submitted**

### Week 4 — Kill losers, double down
- [ ] 🎯💰 **Cut the worst live-transfer vendor; reinvest in the best cost-per-funded-deal**
- [ ] 🏗️ Stand up first SEO cornerstone pages (calculator, "what is an MCA," 1–2 city pages)
- [ ] 📱 Start founder short-form video (2–3 clips): "what funders look at," "signs you're over-leveraged"
- [ ] 📊 **Phase 1 review:** Is cost-per-funded-deal < $1,500? Are VCF files converting?
- [ ] **Target (cumulative): 2 MCA funded + 1 VCF file. Unit economics proven.**

## PHASE 2 — LAYER PAID + START COMPOUNDING (Weeks 5–12)

### Week 5–6 — Turn on paid acquisition
- [ ] 🎯💰 Launch **Google Ads** (your 5-campaign plan: growth metros, TX, Spanish, DC/startup, S. FL)
- [ ] 💰 Launch **Meta retargeting** on website visitors + dead-lead Custom Audiences (pairs with Sequence F)
- [ ] ⚖️ Run Meta on a **dedicated Business Manager** (expect disablement); landing pages clean ("working capital," disclosures)
- [ ] 🏗️ Publish 6 city pages + 2 SEO articles; keep 3–5 videos/week
- [ ] **Target: 4 MCA + 3 VCF (monthly run-rate)**

### Week 7–8 — Scale UCC + first hire trigger
- [ ] 🎯 **If funding 5+ deals/mo AND losing leads to capacity → recruit Closer #1** (1099, 50% company-lead split)
- [ ] 💰 Scale stacked-UCC dialing for VCF (this is the $1M lever)
- [ ] 💰 Begin **LinkedIn outbound** to recruit future Sub-ISOs (Year-2 revenue, start now)
- [ ] 📊 Compare blended cost-per-funded-deal across all live channels
- [ ] **Target: 6 MCA + 5 VCF**

### Week 9–10 — Onboard closer, systematize
- [ ] 🏗️ Onboard Closer #1: scripts, GHL access, round-robin, recordings reviewed
- [ ] 💰 Document the winning live-transfer + VCF playbooks so they're repeatable
- [ ] 📱 Double down on the best-performing organic content format
- [ ] **Target: 7 MCA + 6 VCF**

### Week 11–12 — Phase 2 review
- [ ] 📊 **Quarter review:** booked revenue vs. plan; which channel has the best cost-per-funded-deal?
- [ ] 💰 Raise VCF average file size (target larger stacked accounts, $12–14K commission)
- [ ] 🏗️ SEO should show first signs of life (6–25 organic leads/mo emerging)
- [ ] **Target (run-rate): 8 MCA + 7 VCF/mo. ~$100K booked/mo.**

## PHASE 3 — SCALE TO THE GOAL (Weeks 13–26)

### Week 13–16 — Second closer + VA
- [ ] 🎯 **Closer #2** when lead flow >100/mo and Closer #1 at capacity
- [ ] 🏗️ **Hire a VA** when you + closers spend 30%+ time on doc-chasing/admin
- [ ] 💰 Push paid budget to best channels; expand UCC volume for VCF
- [ ] 📊 Watch the Golden Ratio: cost-per-funded-deal < $1,500, commission > $4,000
- [ ] **Target: 10 MCA + 9 VCF/mo**

### Week 17–20 — Compounding kicks in
- [ ] 💰 SEO/organic now contributing inbound (40+ leads/mo trajectory) — lowers blended CPL
- [ ] 💰 Renewal pipeline opens (Sequence E) — first funded clients hit 40–60% paydown
- [ ] 📱 LinkedIn/organic producing Sub-ISO conversations
- [ ] **Target: 11 MCA + 10 VCF/mo**

### Week 21–26 — Push for $1M
- [ ] 🎯💰 Maximize VCF (biggest tickets) — this is what closes the gap to $1M
- [ ] 🏗️ Consider a **Sales Manager** if 3+ closers and you need off the floor
- [ ] 📊 **6-month review:** booked vs. collected (remember VCF annuitizes); recalc next-6-month plan
- [ ] **Target: 12 MCA + 11 VCF/mo. Cumulative ~$500–550K base / up to ~$1M stretch (booked).**

## The 5 things that make or break the number
1. **Work your own book for VCF first** — free, highest ROI, biggest tickets.
2. **Get VCF's rate card in writing** — the $1M stretch depends on it.
3. **Fix the follow-up leak** (<60s pickup + doc collection) — turns 3% into 8%.
4. **Track cost-per-funded-deal, not cost-per-lead** — kill losers fast.
5. **Run both engines in parallel** — MCA pays the bills, VCF gets you to $1M.

## Don't-do list (saves money)
- ❌ Cold Meta lead-gen at scale · ❌ LinkedIn *paid* for $50K MCA · ❌ mass thin city pages · ❌ expecting SEO revenue in Q1 · ❌ "reverse consolidation" framing.
