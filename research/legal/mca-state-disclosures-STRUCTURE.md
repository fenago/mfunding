## How to use this document
1. Determine the **merchant's state** and the **product type** (MCA / sales-based financing vs. loan product vs. VCF debt relief).
2. Pull the corresponding state block below.
3. Insert the attorney-provided, current statutory disclosure text and deliver it at the time the law requires (commonly **at or before the time a specific offer is extended/consummated**).
4. Capture merchant acknowledgment/signature where required and set the GHL **"Disclosure Acknowledged"** field.

## Disclosure-timing principle (general)
For commercial-financing disclosure states, the disclosure is typically required **when a specific offer of financing is presented** (pipeline stage *Offer Presented*), not at lead capture. Confirm exact triggers per state with counsel.
