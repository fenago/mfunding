# Master Compliance Policy — TCPA, DNC & Litigator-Scrub SOP
### [COMPANY] (Momentum Funding)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]  ·  **Owner:** [COMPLIANCE OWNER NAME/TITLE]  ·  **Applies to:** all employees, 1099 closers, VAs, and contractors performing outreach.

## 1. Purpose & Scope
Protect consumers and Company by ensuring all telephone, SMS, and email outreach for MCA/business funding and VCF debt-relief complies with the TCPA, FCC/FTC rules, the National Do-Not-Call Registry, state telemarketing/calling laws, and applicable advertising/financial-disclosure law.

## 2. Consent Basis (call/text only with a lawful basis)
- **Prior express (or prior express written) consent** captured at lead/form/calculator submission with documented text, timestamp, IP, and source; **or**
- **Established business relationship** (existing CRM contacts — declined applicants, prior/current merchants); **or**
- A number properly **scrubbed** against National DNC + internal DNC + litigator lists where the contact basis requires it.
- Retain consent/relationship proof for at least **[RETENTION PERIOD — e.g., 5 years]**.

## 3. Autodialer / Prerecorded / SMS Rules
- No autodialer, prerecorded/artificial-voice calls, or ringless voicemail to **cell phones** without the consent the law requires. **Manually dial cells** where required.
- SMS programs include program name, opt-out ("Reply STOP"), help ("HELP"), and "msg & data rates may apply."
- Honor **STOP/opt-out immediately** (same business day); propagate across all systems and the internal DNC.

## 4. Calling Hours
Place calls/texts only **8:00 a.m.–9:00 p.m. in the recipient's local time zone**, subject to **stricter state limits** (maintain a state-restrictions table; counsel to provide).

## 5. Do-Not-Call (DNC) Program
- Maintain an **internal DNC list**; add any opt-out within one business day; never call/text internal-DNC numbers.
- Subscribe to and scrub against the **National DNC Registry** as required.
- Provide DNC requests honoring and a written DNC policy on request.

## 6. Litigator / DNC Scrub SOP
1. Before any campaign or dialing session, run the list through the **litigator + DNC + known-complainer scrub** tool ([VENDOR]).
2. Remove flagged numbers (serial litigators, DNC, disconnected, reassigned-number hits).
3. Use a **reassigned-number** check where applicable.
4. Only load **scrubbed** lists into the dialer/GHL; closers may not import personal/unscrubbed lists.
5. Log scrub date, vendor, and list ID for each campaign.

## 7. Product-Language & Advertising Compliance
- **MCA is never a "loan"** — use advance/funding/working capital/purchase of future receivables. Loan products use standard terms.
- **No upfront fees**; "no credit impact" only for review (soft), not formal submission.
- **VCF debt relief:** no guaranteed savings/approval; "**not a law firm**"; substantiate any savings range; lead with no upfront fees.
- Deliver required **state commercial-financing / debt-relief disclosures** (see structure doc); set "Disclosure Acknowledged."

## 8. Training & Acknowledgment
All outreach personnel must sign the TCPA & Compliance Acknowledgment and Code of Conduct before contacting consumers, and re-acknowledge on material policy changes and at least annually.

## 9. Monitoring & Recordkeeping
- Record calls where lawful; perform periodic **QA / script-adherence** review.
- Maintain logs of consent, opt-outs, scrubs, disclosures delivered, and complaints.
- Track and investigate complaints; remediate root causes.

## 10. Violations
Violations may result in retraining, suspension of dialing privileges, withheld commissions on non-compliant deals, or termination, and may trigger indemnification obligations.

## 11. Review
This policy is reviewed at least **annually** and upon legal/regulatory change, by [COMPLIANCE OWNER] with counsel.
