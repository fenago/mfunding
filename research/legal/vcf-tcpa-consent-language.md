# TCPA / Contact Consent Language — Debt-Relief (Distressed Merchant) Outreach
### [COMPANY] (Momentum Funding) — Client-Facing

> Distressed-merchant outreach often targets your **existing CRM contacts** (declined applicants, funded-but-struggling merchants). Most of that is permissible on a **prior business relationship** basis with proper opt-out handling. New/cold contact and any automated calling/texting to cells require the consent below. Confirm with counsel.

## Short Form (calculator / lead form checkbox)
> [ ] By checking this box and providing my number, I agree that [COMPANY] (Momentum Funding) and its representatives may contact me — including by **autodialer, prerecorded/artificial voice, SMS text, and email** — about debt-relief and business-financing options. **Consent is not a condition of any service.** Msg & data rates may apply; frequency varies. Reply **STOP** to opt out, **HELP** for help. I agree to the [Privacy Policy] and [Terms].

## Expanded Form (intake / hardship form)
> I confirm I am the business owner or authorized representative and the contact information is mine. I consent to receive communications from [COMPANY] and the debt-relief Program provider regarding my request and related options, at the number(s)/email provided, including by **automatic telephone dialing systems, prerecorded/artificial voice, SMS/MMS, and email**, even if my number is on a Do-Not-Call list. **I understand consent is not required to receive a free review** and I may revoke it anytime by replying **STOP** to texts or contacting [PHONE/EMAIL]. I understand [COMPANY] is **not a law firm** and makes **no guarantees** of savings, approval, or outcome.

## SMS Program Disclosure
> [COMPANY] SMS: messages about your debt-relief inquiry. Frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply **STOP** to cancel, **HELP** for help. See [Privacy Policy] / [Terms].

## Operational Practices (internal — distressed outreach)
- Call/text only contacts with a **prior business relationship** or proper consent; document the basis.
- **Manual-dial cells**; no autodialer/prerecorded/ringless voicemail to cells without required consent.
- Honor **STOP/opt-out immediately**; propagate to internal DNC. Scrub against National DNC + internal DNC + litigator lists.
- Respect **quiet hours (8a–9p local)** and stricter state limits.
- In messaging: lead with **"no upfront fees,"** make **no guarantees**, say **"not a law firm"** where relevant, and never call the merchant's existing MCA a "loan."
