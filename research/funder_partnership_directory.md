# Funder Partnership Directory

**Compiled:** July 2026 · **For:** MFunding (ISO/broker funder relationships)
**Sources:** FunderIntel's ~200-funder direct-funder directory + web research (FireCrawl) for marketplaces, ISO/white-label programs, and low-revenue specialists.

> Also live in the app at **Admin → Funder Directory** (`/admin/funder-directory`) — searchable/filterable, with a per-row **"Add to lenders"** button where you pick the status (Prospect / Applied / Live / etc.). Nothing is written to your lender database until you click.

## How to read this

- **Low-rev** = accepts low-revenue / weak-credit / thin files (funds **C/D paper**). **This is where your "declined for low revenue" deals should go.**
- **Apply-once** = one application reaches many funders (marketplace / portal).
- **White-label** = you can fund under *your own* brand.
- **ISO** = has a broker/ISO/partner deal-submission program.
- **Paper grade:** A = prime (700+) → B (600s) → C (500s) → D (below 500 / stacked / distressed).
