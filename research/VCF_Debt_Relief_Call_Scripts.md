# VCF Debt Relief — Own-Book Outreach Scripts
### Calling your declined / stacked / struggling merchants · June 2026

**Who you're calling:** business owners already in your CRM — declined MCA applicants, merchants you funded who are now stacked or struggling, and anyone who went cold because the payments got tight. These are warm: you have a prior relationship and they know the name. This is the highest-converting, zero-cost VCF channel.

**The offer (white-label — do NOT say "Value Capital Funding"):** two programs — (1) **attorney-led debt restructuring** (no new loan; lowers daily/weekly payments 50–75%; negotiates your existing advances), and (2) an **FDIC bank term loan** that refinances your advances into one affordable monthly payment.

---

## ⚖️ Compliance guardrails (read before dialing)
- **Tone = help, not hype.** No guarantees. Say "may," "could," "many clients," "depending on your situation."
- **Never promise** a specific reduction %, approval, or outcome. "50–75%" is a *typical* range, not a promise — and must be substantiated.
- **Lead with "no upfront fees."** It's true and it's the #1 trust signal.
- **TCPA/DNC:** only call numbers you have a prior business relationship with or that are scrubbed; honor opt-outs immediately; manual dial cells; no auto-dialer/RVM to cells without consent.
- **Don't call it a "loan"** when describing the MCA they already have ("advance/funding"). The FDIC refinance *is* a loan — that term is fine for that product.
- **Don't overstate attorney involvement** beyond "an attorney-led team."
- **"Reverse consolidation" — don't pitch it.** Lead with restructuring + FDIC refinance.

## SCRIPT 1 — Declined applicant (you couldn't fund them)

**Opener**
> "Hey [First Name], it's [Your Name] from Momentum Funding — we spoke a while back when you were looking for working capital. Do you have 30 seconds? … I'm not calling to sell you an advance. Honestly, the reason a lot of folks like you got a 'no' is they're already carrying advances that are eating their cash flow. I started helping business owners in exactly that spot get those payments *down*. Can I ask — are you currently making daily or weekly payments on any advances right now?"

**If yes →** go to **Qualifying** below.
**If no →** "Got it — are the payments manageable, or is cash flow tight some weeks?" (If tight, continue. If genuinely fine, mark for renewal nurture and exit politely.)

---

## SCRIPT 2 — Stacked / struggling client (you funded them, now they're underwater)

**Opener**
> "Hey [First Name], [Your Name] from Momentum. I've been checking in with the business owners we've worked with, because a lot of people took on more than one advance over the past year and the daily payments got brutal. No judgment at all — it's incredibly common. How's cash flow holding up for you right now?"

**If they admit stress →**
> "That's exactly why I'm calling. We now have a way to help — an attorney-led team that renegotiates your existing advances so your daily or weekly payments come *way* down, often 50 to 75%, without taking on a new loan. There are no upfront fees. Can I ask you a few quick questions to see if it's even a fit?"

→ go to **Qualifying**.

---

## QUALIFYING (both scripts) — keep it conversational
Capture for the GHL VCF intake / submission to VCF:
1. "Roughly how many advances or positions are you carrying right now?" (need the stacking picture)
2. "Ballpark, what's the total balance across all of them?" (**$50K+ total debt = qualifies**)
3. "What are you paying combined per day or per week?"
4. "Who are the funders, if you remember?"
5. "And what's making it hardest right now — slow season, a big debit, payroll?"

**Qualified if:** $50K+ total MCA/business debt, multiple positions or clear hardship, owner is the decision-maker.

## THE PITCH (after they qualify)
> "Okay — based on what you're telling me, you're a strong fit. Here's how it works, simply: an attorney-led team steps in and renegotiates what you owe with your funders, so instead of these crushing daily debits you've got one manageable payment — many clients see it drop 50 to 75%. If you qualify, there's also an option to refinance everything into a single affordable monthly bank loan. Either way: **no upfront fees**, it's a free review, and you're under no obligation. The only thing I need is a few documents so the team can build you a custom plan — usually within 24 hours. Want me to get that started for you today?"

**Close:** "Great — I'll text you a secure link right now to upload your last few statements, and a specialist will call you within 24 hours with your plan. What's the best number for that text?"

## OBJECTION HANDLING

**"Is this a loan? I don't want more debt."**
> "Great question. The main program is *not* a loan — it actually reduces what you owe by renegotiating your existing advances. There's a separate option to refinance into one bank loan if that fits you better, but nothing's added unless it lowers your total payment. You'll see the full numbers before you decide anything."

**"What does it cost me?"**
> "No upfront, out-of-pocket fees. Any program fee is built into your new, much lower payment — so you're not paying anything extra today to get started. That's the whole point: you've already paid enough."

**"Will this hurt my credit?"**
> "In many situations there's no negative impact to your business or personal credit — but I won't promise specifics; the specialist will walk you through exactly how it works for your case before you commit to anything."

**"How do I know this is legit?"**
> "Totally fair. It's an attorney-led team with 30-plus years of experience that's restructured over $100 million in business debt. The review is free, there are no upfront fees, and you decide at the end with all the numbers in front of you. If it's not a fit, you walk away owing nothing."

**"I'm too busy / call me later."**
> "I hear you — and I know the payments aren't slowing down while you're busy. It's a 24-hour turnaround and the first step is just sending a few statements. Can I text you the link so you can do it whenever you get a minute tonight?"

**"Let me think about it."**
> "Of course. The review is free and there's zero obligation — thinking about it costs nothing, but the daily debits don't stop. How about I send the link, you upload when ready, and a specialist gives you the plan? You can still say no after you see the numbers."
