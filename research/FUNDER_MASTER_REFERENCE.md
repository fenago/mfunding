## 2. Universal submission packet (works for almost every MCA funder)

1. **Completed application** (ours or theirs), signed.
2. **Most recent bank statements** — 3 months minimum; **4 months** for UCS; **4–6 months** for GoKapital. PDF, **all pages, unredacted**.
3. **Owner's driver's license** (front; some want back too).
4. **Voided business check** / bank routing + account (name must match the application).
5. **Subject line = the exact legal or DBA business name.**
6. **CC your ISO rep** so they can shepherd the file.
7. For borderline deals, add a 2–3 line strength summary (cash flow, tenure, amount/term sought).
