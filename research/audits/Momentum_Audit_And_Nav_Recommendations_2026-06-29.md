## Backend Navigation — Audit & Reorg Recommendations

### Why the current nav feels illogical
The admin sidebar groups by loose theme rather than by the *job the user is doing*, which creates real friction:

1. **"Lead Generation" is overloaded (9 items)** — it mixes three different jobs: managing lead *supply* (vendors, scorecard, lists), running *outreach* (campaigns, sequences), and *tools/referrals*. Too much to scan.
2. **The two playbooks are split apart** — "Revenue Playbooks" lives in *Overview* while "Pipeline Playbook" lives in *Training*, with "Resources" also in Training. Learning/how-to content is scattered across three groups.
3. **"Funder Network" mixes unrelated entity types** — it contains Lenders (funders) *plus* Closers (your internal 1099 team) and Sub-ISOs (broker partners). Those are people/partners, not funders.
4. **"Finance" mixes operational money with what-if models** — Commissions (real money owed/paid) sits next to Unit Economics, Live Transfer ROI, Closer Comp, and Business Model, which are planning calculators. Different mental modes.
5. **Related concepts are separated** — "Live Transfer Leads" is under Lead Generation but "Live Transfer ROI" is under Finance; "Lead Sources" (cost/ROI) sits with lead supply but is really analytics.
6. **"Analytics" is a tiny 2-item island** disconnected from the other number-crunching screens.
7. **Nav doesn't match permissions** — closers see nav entries for pages whose tables deny them in RLS (see audit finding #17). Nav should only show what a role can actually use.

### Current structure
| Group | Items | Problem |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Overview | Dashboard, Launch Board, Revenue Playbooks | Playbooks marooned away from other training |
| Pipeline | Customers, Deals, Renewals, Doc Review | OK |
| Lead Generation | Marketing Vendors, Live Transfer Leads, Vendor Scorecard, Lead Lists & Data, Lead Tools, Campaigns, Sequences, Lead Sources, Referrals | Overloaded; 3 jobs in one bucket |
| Finance | Commissions, Unit Economics (MCA), Unit Economics (VCF), Live Transfer ROI, Closer Comp, Business Model | Operational money mixed with planning models |
| Funder Network | Lenders, Funder Guide, Closers, Sub-ISOs | Lenders mixed with internal team + partners |
| Analytics | Analytics, Real-Time | Tiny island |
| Training | Pipeline Playbook, Resources | Other playbook is elsewhere |
| System | Users, Compliance, Integrations, GHL Sync Log, Platform Config, Settings | OK |

### Proposed structure (group by job-to-be-done)
| # | Group | Items | Why this grouping |
|---|-------|-------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Home** | Dashboard, Launch Board | Where you start the day |
| 2 | **Pipeline** | Customers, Deals, Renewals, Doc Review | The live deal flow (unchanged) |
| 3 | **Lead Sourcing** | Marketing Vendors, Vendor Scorecard, Live Transfer Leads, Lead Lists & Data, Lead Sources | Everything about where leads come from + what they cost |
| 4 | **Marketing & Outreach** | Campaigns, Sequences, Lead Tools, Referrals | Active demand-gen + nurture engine |
| 5 | **Team & Partners** | Closers, Sub-ISOs | The people/orgs you work *with* (not funders) |
| 6 | **Funder Network** | Lenders, Funder Guide | Funders only — clean and focused |
| 7 | **Finance** | Commissions *(then)* Unit Economics (MCA), Unit Economics (VCF), Live Transfer ROI, Closer Comp, Business Model | Operational money first, planning models after — or split models into their own "Planning & Models" group if this feels long |
| 8 | **Insights** | Analytics, Real-Time | Live reporting, kept distinct from hypothetical models |
| 9 | **Playbooks & Training** | Revenue Playbooks, Pipeline Playbook, Resources | All how-to/learning in one place |
| 10 | **System** | Users, Compliance, Integrations, GHL Sync Log, Platform Config, Settings | Admin/config (unchanged) |

### Key changes (what moved and why)
- **Split the overloaded "Lead Generation"** into **Lead Sourcing** (supply/cost) and **Marketing & Outreach** (campaigns/nurture). Each becomes scannable.
- **Pull Closers + Sub-ISOs out of "Funder Network"** into a new **Team & Partners** group; Funder Network becomes lenders-only.
- **Consolidate both playbooks + Resources** into **Playbooks & Training** (fixes the split across Overview/Training).
- **Keep Commissions operational**, group the calculators after it (or break them into a dedicated **Planning & Models** group). Renaming "Analytics" → **Insights** signals "live numbers" vs the what-if models.
- **Co-locate Live Transfer Leads with Lead Sourcing and Lead Sources**, so the live-transfer story (supply + cost + ROI) is reachable together.

### Quick wins (low effort, high clarity)
1. Rename + split "Lead Generation" → "Lead Sourcing" + "Marketing & Outreach" (pure regroup in `AdminSidebar.tsx`).
2. Move Closers + Sub-ISOs into a new "Team & Partners" group.
3. Move "Revenue Playbooks" out of Overview into "Playbooks & Training" alongside "Pipeline Playbook" + "Resources".
4. **Align nav with RBAC** (ties to finding #17): only render a nav item if the signed-in role can actually use the destination's data — don't show closers links their RLS blocks.
