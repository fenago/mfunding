## 1. WHAT MFUNDING IS

MFunding, LLC is an ISO (Independent Sales Organization) that brokers Merchant Cash Advances. We connect small businesses needing working capital with funders who purchase a percentage of the merchant's future receivables at a discount. We do NOT fund deals with our own money. We are the broker.

**Critical distinction:** MCAs are NOT loans. They are purchases of future receivables. The merchant remits a daily or weekly retrieval (ACH debit) until the purchased amount plus fees is satisfied. All marketing materials, sales conversations, and follow-up communications must maintain this distinction.

**Industry hierarchy:**
```
Funder (has capital, underwrites, funds deals)
  → pays commission to →
ISO / Broker (MFunding — finds merchants, packages deals, submits to funders)
  → may pay split to →
Sub-ISO or Sales Rep (smaller broker working under our umbrella)
```

## 4. FUNDER ROLODEX — BUILDING YOUR NETWORK

Need 5–7+ funders minimum to submit deals for competing offers. More funders = better approval rates = more funded deals = better standing with funders (virtuous cycle).

### Tier A (A Paper) — 650+ Credit, $15K+ Monthly Revenue, 2+ Years
- Factor rates: 1.10–1.25
- Examples: Rapid Finance, National Funding, Reliant Funding, Newtek
- Best for: Clean deals with strong financials

### Tier B (B Paper) — 550–650 Credit, $10K+ Monthly Revenue, 1+ Years
- Factor rates: 1.25–1.40
- Examples: Meritus Capital, Next Wave Funding, Cobalt Funding
- Best for: Average-credit merchants with decent revenue

### Tier C/D (C/D Paper) — Below 550 Credit, $7,500+ Monthly Revenue, 6+ Months
- Factor rates: 1.35–1.55+
- Examples: NRG (1.20 minimum), River Advance, 24 Capital
- Best for: Harder-to-place deals where you earn more points

### Specialty Funders
- **Capybara Capital:** Whale deals $500K+
- **Redwood:** SBA loans (different product, longer timeline)
- **PayPal Loan Builder:** Online businesses

### Where to Find Funders
- **Funder Intel** (funderintel.com/fundingcompanieslist) — 200+ funders with contact info
- **deBanked** (debanked.com/funder-lender-directory) — industry publication
- **DailyFunder forum** — industry community
- **Synergy Direct Solution's curated list** — included with lead purchases

### Submission Best Practices
- Submit to 3–5 funders simultaneously per deal
- Know each funder's stip requirements before submitting
- Build personal relationships with underwriters (they'll push borderline deals through)
- Track approval rates by funder — drop anyone below 30%
- Match the deal profile to the right funder tier (A-paper to A-funders, etc.)

## 8. COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY

### State Disclosure Requirements (2025–2026)
- **New York:** Commercial Financing Disclosure Law — must provide APR equivalent, total cost, payment amounts
- **California:** SB 1235 — similar commercial financing disclosure requirements
- **Virginia, Utah, Florida, Connecticut, Georgia, Kansas, Texas, Missouri:** Various disclosure and registration requirements

### Federal Compliance
- **CFPB Section 1071:** If arranging ≥100 covered transactions annually, must collect and report demographic data
- **TCPA:** All phone and SMS communications must comply. GHL handles opt-in/opt-out tracking.

### MCA vs Loan Distinction
**Every closer, Sub-ISO, and marketing material must represent MCAs as purchases of future receivables, NOT loans.** Misrepresentation creates regulatory and legal risk.
