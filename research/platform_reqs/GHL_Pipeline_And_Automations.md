# MFunding — GHL Pipeline, Stages & Stage Automations (Build Spec)
### Last updated: June 26, 2026

> **Why this document exists:** The GoHighLevel **API v2 cannot create pipelines or stages** (only `GET /opportunities/pipelines` exists — a `POST` returns `401 "not authorized for this scope"`). Pipelines must be built in the **GHL UI** or generated by **GHL's Workflow AI assistant**. This doc is the build spec: the pipeline, every stage, the automation that fires on each stage, the transition rules, and a **ready-to-paste prompt for GHL AI** at the bottom.

---

Our app's `ghl-sync` edge function maps a deal's status to a GHL stage **by matching the stage name (case-insensitive)**. If the names don't match, opportunities fall back to the first stage. Create the stages with these **exact** names, in this order:

**Pipeline name:** `MFunding Deal Pipeline`
**Product-neutral language** throughout (MCA ≠ loan): use "funding," "capital," "advance," "working capital." Never "loan" in MCA messaging.

## Pipelines

**Primary — `MFunding Deal Pipeline`** (the 10 stages above). All product types (MCA, term loan, SBA, LOC, equipment) flow through this single pipeline; the difference is which funders receive the submission and which stips/disclosures apply.

**Optional — `Renewal Pipeline`** (advanced, later): a separate board for funded merchants tracked by paydown %. For now, renewals are handled by **Stage 10 (Renewal Eligible)** + the paydown trigger from our app, so a second pipeline is NOT required to start. (Note: our sync targets the first pipeline; keep `MFunding Deal Pipeline` as the only/primary pipeline until we add multi-pipeline routing.)

## Stage-by-stage: automations & transitions

Each stage lists: **on-entry automation**, the **named follow-up sequence** (from the master plan, Sequences A–F), the **exit condition**, and where it goes next (incl. the "cold" branch).

### 1) New Lead
- **On entry:** Instant auto-response (SMS + email, <1 min); assign via round-robin to a closer; AI Employee opens a 2-way SMS pre-qualification; create task "Call within 5 min"; missed-call text-back armed.
- **Sequence:** speed-to-lead burst (Day 0: SMS + call task).
- **Exit → Contacted:** when a 2-way conversation starts or the lead answers a call.
- **Cold branch → (stays) / No-answer:** if no contact, begin **Sequence B (No Answer, 7 days)**; after Day 7 breakup → tag `nurture-60` and move to **Dead/Lost**.

### 2) Contacted
- **On entry:** Log the conversation; AI/closer runs intro; book qualification call if needed.
- **Sequence:** continue **Sequence B** branches if calls go unanswered.
- **Exit → Qualifying:** when a real conversation is established and you begin BANT-F.

### 3) Qualifying
- **On entry:** Capture **BANT-F** into custom fields (Monthly Revenue, Time in Business, Funding Amount, Industry, Product Type). Auto-disqualify if below thresholds (e.g. <6 mo in business or <$15k/mo).
- **Sequence:** if "not right now" → **Sequence C (Soft No, 90 days → quarterly)**.
- **Exit → Application Sent:** when qualified.
- **Cold branch:** disqualified → tag + **Dead/Lost**; soft-no → Sequence C nurture.

### 4) Application Sent
- **On entry:** Send the **application form/funnel link** (intake lives in GHL) + trigger the **compliance disclosure** step (send state+product-specific disclosure, capture `Disclosure Acknowledged`). Reminder cadence to complete the app (Day 0, +4h, Day 1).
- **Exit → Docs Collected:** when the application is submitted.

### 5) Docs Collected  ⭐ #1 funnel leak
- **On entry:** Fire **Sequence A (Stips/Docs, 14 days — HIGHEST PRIORITY)**: Day 0 SMS with **Plaid link** + photo/email alternatives; +2h second SMS; Day 1 call+SMS; Day 2 three-methods SMS; Day 4 call+VM+SMS; Day 7 urgency; Day 10 email; Day 14 breakup. Set `Plaid Connected` when bank is linked.
- **Exit → Submitted to Funder:** when stips are complete (bank statements via Plaid/manual, ID, app, voided check, cred auth).
- **Cold branch:** Day 14 breakup → `nurture-30`.

### 6) Submitted to Funder
- **On entry:** Notify VA/closer. **(Our app does the work here:** internal underwriting scorecard + funder matching + multi-funder submission to 3–5 funders.) GHL just tracks state + notifies.
- **Exit → Offer Received:** when a funder responds with terms.
- **Cold branch:** all declines → **Sequence D** prep / resubmit.

### 7) Offer Received
- **On entry:** Internal — assemble best offers (factor rate, term, amount, daily/weekly payment) for side-by-side presentation (our app builds the comparison).
- **Exit → Offer Presented:** when offers are shown to the merchant.

### 8) Offer Presented
- **On entry:** Send branded **offer link**; present **2+ options**; create "follow up on offer" task.
- **Sequence:** if declined → **Sequence D (Offer Declined, 45 days)**: Day 0 objection call → resubmit to different funders → Day 1–3 new options → Day 7 one more → Day 14 breakup → Day 45 re-engage.
- **Exit → Funded:** when an offer is accepted and money is deposited.

### 9) Funded
- **On entry:** **Sequence E (Funded → Renewal)** kickoff: Day 1 congrats + Google review request + referral ask ($100 gift card). Mark `funded_at`. (Our app calculates commission + payout.)
- **Exit → Renewal Eligible:** when paydown reaches the first milestone (our app fires this via the `ghl-webhook`/trigger at 40%).

### 10) Renewal Eligible
- **On entry / paydown triggers (driven by our app's paydown math):**
  - **40% paydown:** "You may qualify for additional capital" SMS.
  - **60%:** call + SMS with renewal offer.
  - **75%:** "best time to renew — most favorable terms."
  - **100%:** direct call for next round.
- **Exit:** new application → re-enter pipeline at **Application Sent** (or New Lead) as a renewal deal.

### Cross-pipeline: Sequence F (Mass Reactivation)
A standalone workflow (not a stage): monthly blast to the `dead`/`nurture` tags, rotating 3 message templates. Expected 3–5% re-engagement → re-enters at **New Lead**.

### The generic per-lender submission flow (each funder's workflow follows this)
**Step 1 — Submit the package (email).** Trigger: a `deal_submissions` row is created for this funder (status `submitted`) / opportunity tagged `submit:<funder>`. Action: send the package email to the funder's address (+ CC), subject `Deal Submission — {{business_name}} — {{amount_requested}}`, body = standard cover note, attachments: **signed application + 3 months bank statements (+ any required stips)**. Stamp `submitted_at`.
**Step 2 — Confirm + start the clock.** ~4 business hours later: check for a bounce/auto-reply; if it bounced, alert ops. Start the response SLA.
**Step 3 — Day 1, no decision.** Send a short follow-up email **and** create a task to call the underwriter (use the funder's phone).
**Step 4 — Day 2.** Call/SMS the underwriter task again (relationships move borderline deals).
**Step 5 — On response (branch):**
- **Approved / Offer** → move deal to **Offer Received**, capture terms (amount, factor, term, payment, commission pts) on the `deal_submission`, notify the closer.
- **Stips requested** → send the requested docs, stay in stage, reset the timer.
- **Declined** → record decline reason on the submission; if **all** funders declined → fire **Sequence D** and resubmit to the next-tier funder.
**Step 6 — Day 4–5, no response** → escalate (manager call), mark the submission `stale`.

### Division of labor
- 🟣 **GHL** sends the emails, runs the timers/follow-ups, and the per-funder routing (incl. Reliant's high-risk → The LCF Group branch).
- 🔵 **Our app** assembles the package (application + Plaid/manual bank data + stips) and writes the `deal_submissions` rows; the multi-funder submission UI (Phase 4) is what kicks these workflows off.

## Product Line 2 — Value Capital Funding (VCF): MCA Debt Relief / Consolidation
**This is a separate product line, not an MCA submission.** VCF serves merchants who are **over-leveraged with multiple active MCAs and in financial distress** — we bail them out by **renegotiating, consolidating, or refinancing** their stacked positions into a single, more manageable arrangement. Different customer, different funnel, different tone (relief, not sales), different compliance posture.

- **Submission contact:** `partnerprogram@valuecapitalfunding.com` (Ferne Kornfeld).
- **Target customer:** 2+ active MCA positions, high combined daily/weekly debits, cash-flow distressed, possibly behind or defaulting.
- **Lead sources:** UCC filings showing multiple positions; distressed-merchant lists; inbound "I'm drowning in MCA payments."

### Draft VCF pipeline + automation (🟡 CONFIRM stages/criteria/commission with owner)
1. **New Lead (Distressed)** → empathetic intro; do NOT cross-sell a new MCA.
2. **Hardship Consultation** → understand position count, total balances, daily/weekly debits, urgency.
3. **Positions & Balances Analysis** → collect **all current MCA agreements** + **bank statements showing the debits**; tally exposure. (This is the VCF equivalent of "Docs Collected".)
4. **Strategy / Proposal** → consolidate vs refinance vs renegotiate; present the relief plan.
5. **Agreement Sent** → e-sign the VCF engagement/agreement.
6. **Submitted to VCF** → email package to `partnerprogram@valuecapitalfunding.com` (same email-flow shape as Product Line 1: send → SLA → follow-up → branch on response).
7. **Restructure Executed** → positions paid off/settled, single new arrangement in place.
8. **Servicing / Monitoring** → ensure the merchant stays on track; renewal/referral later.

**Compliance note:** debt-relief/consolidation messaging is sensitive — no guarantees of savings/approval, separate disclosures, still "purchase of future receivables" / never "loan." Confirm whether any state debt-settlement rules apply.

> 🟡 **For the owner:** confirm the VCF pipeline stages, qualification thresholds (min positions / min combined debits), the exact stips list, and the commission model — then this becomes a second GHL pipeline (`MFunding VCF Pipeline`) separate from the MCA pipeline.

## After GHL builds it
1. In our app, open any Deal and click **Sync to GHL** — it will now place the opportunity in the correct stage (names match). Or call the `ghl-sync` function with `{"action":"pipelines"}` to confirm the new pipeline + stage IDs.
2. Register the inbound webhook (`/functions/v1/ghl-webhook?secret=...`) for Opportunity + Contact events so stage moves in GHL flow back to our `deals`/`customers`.
3. Wire the paydown trigger from our app to set the GHL `Paydown %` field (drives Renewal Triggers).
