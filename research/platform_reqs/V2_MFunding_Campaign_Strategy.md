# SECTION 6: SCALING DECISION TREE

**Close rate > 8% and cost per funded deal < $1,200:**
→ Scale up. Increase live transfers 25%. Increase Google Ads 30%.

**Close rate 5–8% and cost per funded deal $1,200–$1,800:**
→ Optimize. Focus on stips collection, test new sequences, review funder matches.

**Close rate < 5% and cost per funded deal > $2,000:**
→ Diagnose. Lead quality (test new vendor)? Follow-up (audit sequences)? Funders (add more)? One market underperforming (pause campaign)?

**When to hire first closer:**
→ Consistently funding 5+ deals/month AND losing leads due to capacity. Never hire ahead of lead supply. Each closer needs 40–50 leads/month.
