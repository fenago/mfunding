Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need?
We work with business owners across the credit spectrum. While some of our partners require 650+, we have options for scores as low as 500. Your monthly revenue and time in business matter more than your credit score.
How fast can I get funded?
Many clients receive offers within hours and capital within 24–48 hours of accepting an offer. Our instant bank verification technology speeds up the process significantly.
Are there any upfront fees?
No. There are never any upfront fees to apply or receive offers. Our service is completely free to you — we're compensated by our funding partners.
Will applying affect my credit score?
No. The initial application and matching process does not involve a hard credit pull. A credit check may occur only after you choose to move forward with a specific offer.
What documents will I need?
Most of our partners just need to verify your business bank account — which you can do instantly through our secure portal. Some programs may also request basic identification. We'll walk you through everything.
