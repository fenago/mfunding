### Phase 2 Recommended Approach

**Week 1-2: Practice Phase**
- [ ] Purchase 50-100 aged leads from Synergy ($50-100)
- [ ] Practice pitch and learn objections
- [ ] Track conversion to appointments set

**Week 3-4: Appointment Phase**
- [ ] Purchase 10-20 appointment leads ($150-400)
- [ ] Improve close rate with better quality leads

**Week 5+: Live Transfer Phase**
- [ ] Start with 5 live transfers/week ($375-750/week)
- [ ] Track conversion rate meticulously
- [ ] Scale what works, cut what doesn't

### Questions to Ask Lead Vendors Before Buying

- [ ] Are leads exclusive or shared?
- [ ] What's your return/replacement policy for bad leads?
- [ ] What qualification criteria do you use?
- [ ] Do you scrub against TCPA litigator lists?
- [ ] What's your minimum order?
- [ ] Can I do a small test batch first?
